Welcome to my bank test. To run, please:
  • open your Developer Toolbar (in chrome, `functionKey + F12 key`)
  • Navigate to your browser's console
  • enter the following into the console:
    • account = new BankAccount()
    • console.log(account.printBankStatement())
    • account.recordTransaction({date:'10/02/2012' , type: 'deposit', transactionAmount: 300})
    • account.recordTransaction({date:'10/02/2012' , type: 'withdraw', transactionAmount: 20})
    • console.log(account.printBankStatement())

    The expected output of the first console.log:

              date || credit || debit || balance
          undefined

    The expected output of the second console.log:

              date || credit || debit || balance
          10/02/2012 || || 20.00 || 280.00
          10/02/2012 || 300.00 || || 300.00
