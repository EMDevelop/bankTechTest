Welcome to my bank test. To run, please:

  • open your Developer Toolbar (in chrome, `functionKey + F12 key`)
  • Navigate to your browser's console
  • enter the following into the console:
    • app = new BankingApp()
    • app.makeDeposit({date: '10/03/2023', transactionAmount: 1000.00})
    • console.log(app.printMyAccountStatement())
      •             date || credit || debit || balance
            10/03/2023 || 1000.00 || || 1000.00
    • app.makeWithdrawal({date: '10/03/2023', transactionAmount: 30.99})
    • console.log(app.printMyAccountStatement())
      •             date || credit || debit || balance
            10/03/2023 || || 30.99 || 969.01
            10/03/2023 || 1000.00 || || 1000.00
